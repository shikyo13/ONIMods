[h1]BuildThrough[/h1]

In vanilla ONI, duplicants refuse to build or deconstruct anything if a solid tile stands between them and the construction site. Want to upgrade an insulated tile behind a wall? You have to tear things apart, build temporary ladders, or rearrange your base just so a dupe can reach the errand. For something as simple as swapping a tile, that's a lot of busywork.

BuildThrough fixes this. Dupes can now build and deliver materials through solid tiles, and deconstruct buildings on the other side of walls. No more dismantling half your base for a simple upgrade.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Selective bypass[/b] — only construction and deconstruction errands reach through walls; normal dupe pathing is completely unaffected
[*] [b]No gameplay exploits[/b] — dupes still need to carry materials and path to an adjacent cell; this just removes the "line of sight through solid" restriction on work errands
[*] [b]Graceful fallback[/b] — if a game update changes the targeted code, the mod logs a warning and falls back to vanilla behavior instead of crashing
[*] [b]Configurable[/b] — single Enabled toggle in PLib mod options (restart required)
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
Works with the base game and all DLCs (Spaced Out!, The Frosty Planet Pack). Does not modify save data.
