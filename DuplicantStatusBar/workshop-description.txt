[h1]Duplicant Status Bar[/h1]

A persistent bar at the top of your screen showing every duplicant on the current asteroid. Think RimWorld's colonist bar, but for ONI.

Vanilla gives you a global stress number in the corner and that's about it. No persistent per-dupe status, no way to spot a suffocating dupe or an imminent breakdown without clicking through menus or hunting someone down on the map. By the time you notice, they're already destroying your oxygen diffusers. This mod fixes that.

Each dupe gets a widget with a stress-colored border that shifts through a 5-tier gradient: green at calm, through lime, yellow, orange, up to pulsing red at 80%+. A health fill bar drains from top to bottom as dupes take damage, with a dark overlay making injuries immediately visible. Up to 3 alert badges can appear simultaneously on each portrait for critical states like suffocation, low health, scalding, hypothermia, starvation, radiation sickness, bladder emergencies, and disease. Alerts are priority-ordered so the most dangerous condition always shows first.

Click any widget to select that dupe and snap the camera to them. Hover for a detailed tooltip showing their current task, stress, health, breath, bladder, and body temperature, plus any active alerts — the tooltip stays on-screen even when hovering dupes near the edges.

Displays dupe portraits by default, with a colored-initials mode available in mod settings for a lighter look or better readability in packed colonies. The bar wraps into multiple rows as your colony grows, with a scrollbar when rows exceed the configured limit. Drag the header to reposition anywhere on screen, or drag the resize grip to adjust portrait size on the fly.

[h2]Features[/h2]
[list]
[*] [b]Stress-colored borders[/b] — 5-tier gradient from green (calm) to pulsing red (critical), with configurable thresholds
[*] [b]Health fill bar[/b] — vertical drain with green-to-red gradient and a dark damage overlay above the portrait
[*] [b]Alert badges[/b] — suffocating, low HP, scalding, hypothermia, irradiated, starving, disease, bladder urgent, overstressed, overjoyed, stuck, idle; up to 3 shown simultaneously with priority ordering and distinct colors across the full spectrum
[*] [b]Hover tooltip[/b] — name, current task, stress, health, breath, bladder, body temperature, and all active alerts color-coded
[*] [b]Click to select[/b] — selects the dupe and snaps the camera to them
[*] [b]Portraits or initials[/b] — full dupe portraits by default; switch to colored initials in mod settings
[*] [b]Sorting[/b] — by stress level, alphabetical, or job role
[*] [b]Multi-row grid layout[/b] — wraps into rows automatically, with configurable max width and max rows
[*] [b]Scroll overflow[/b] — thin scrollbar appears when dupes exceed visible rows
[*] [b]Draggable & collapsible[/b] — drag the header to reposition; collapse to just the header bar; position saved across sessions
[*] [b]Resize grip[/b] — drag the bottom-right corner to adjust portrait size (16–96px), persisted between sessions
[*] [b]Configurable[/b] — portrait size, bar opacity, max width, max rows, display mode, stress thresholds, and individual alert toggles via PLib mod options
[/list]

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]
Works with the base game and all DLCs (Spaced Out!, The Frosty Planet Pack). Display only — does not modify any game logic or save data.
